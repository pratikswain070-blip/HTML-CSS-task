What are grid lines?
Invisible lines between columns. Like borders you can't see. We count them to position boxes.

How many columns does 1 / 3 span?
2 columns line 1 to line 3 = 2 boxes wide.

What happens to other boxes?
They move out of the way. Grid rearranges everything automatically.

Can boxes overlap?
YES 

Where do we use column span?
News sites (big article + small ads). Pinterest (big and small images). Dashboards.
