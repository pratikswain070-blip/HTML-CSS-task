    What does fr stand for?
Fr means "fraction". It divides the space equally among items.

How many columns are created?
3 columns are created because we wrote three 1fr values.

What happens when screen size is reduced?
The columns get narrower but stay equal. 

Do items overflow or adjust?
Items adjust and get smaller to fit inside the columns.

Why is fr better than px for layouts?
Fr is better because px is fixed size.
